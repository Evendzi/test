SATHYA EDUCARE COMPETENCY TRUST

Sathya Educare Competency Trust, A non-profit Organisation headquartered in Bangalore, is dedicated to a noble mission of transforming lives in various regions. With a dual focus on empowering job seekers and enhancing the well-being of senior citizens, our organization is deeply committed to making a profound impact on elder care services..

Our trust goes beyond addressing unemployment to improve the quality of elder care services. Recognizing the growing need for compassionate and skilled caregivers, we focus on enhancing individuals' capabilities in providing care to senior citizens. Through specialized training programs, caregivers are equipped with the knowledge and empathy required to ensure that elderly individuals lead fulfilling lives with dignity and respect.

Collaborative Assessment: We collaborate and assess elder care centers across regions twice a year to identify gaps and offer support where needed.
Improved Service Quality: With well-trained staff from our program, your care center can deliver higher quality service to your clients.
Hassle-Free Training: We act as your training center, saving you the effort of organizing and conducting training sessions.
Guidance and Consultation: We provide guidance through consultation to enhance geriatric service methodology.
Retention Support: Candidates who undergo our training understand the value of their service, resulting in high retention rates.
Certification: Upon completion, participants receive a Geriatric Care Specialist certification, validating their expertise and improving the quality of work at your care center.
